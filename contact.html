Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you provide a quote?

We typically provide quotes within 24–48 hours of your initial enquiry. For larger projects, we may arrange a site visit first.

Do you offer free quotes?

Absolutely. All quotes are completely free and carry no obligation. We give you a clear, itemised, fixed-price estimate.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes — we are fully licensed plastering contractors and carry comprehensive public liability insurance on every job.

What areas do you service?

We service your city and all surrounding suburbs within a 40km radius. Contact us to confirm your specific area.

How long does a typical job take?

A patch repair may take hours; a full house plaster can take several days. We'll give you a clear timeline upfront.

Do you clean up after the job?

Always. Dust sheets are used throughout and all debris is removed upon completion. We leave your space clean and tidy.
