About Dandy Plastering

Melbourne's Trusted Plastering Specialists

Dandy Plastering is a Melbourne-based business serving residential, commercial and industrial clients across the city and surrounding suburbs. Based in Narre Warren, we bring meticulous attention to every project — big or small.

We believe plastering is more than a trade — it's an art form. Every wall we touch reflects our passion for precision and pride in our craft.
