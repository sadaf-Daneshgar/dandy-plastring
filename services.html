What We Offer

Dandy Plastering offers a comprehensive range of services for every project — from a single patch repair to a full commercial build across Melbourne. Every job gets the same care, professionalism and quality.

Residential Plastering

Your home deserves the finest finish. We work closely with homeowners on new builds, renovations and repairs to deliver walls and ceilings you'll be proud of for years to come.

Whether it's a single room, a full house replaster, or a tricky patch repair, we bring the same level of precision and care to every residential project.

  • New plasterboard installation & set
  • Ceiling repairs & cornices
  • Patch & repair work
  • Smooth & texture finishes
  • Heritage & period home restoration

Commercial Plastering

We understand commercial projects demand fast turnarounds, minimal disruption, and impeccable quality. Our experienced team is equipped to handle large-scale commercial plastering from the ground up.

From boutique retail fitouts to multi-storey office buildings — we have the capacity and expertise to deliver.

  • Office & retail fitouts
  • Hospitals & educational facilities

  • Gyprock partition walls

Industrial Plastering

Industrial environments require robust, long-lasting finishes that can withstand tough conditions. We specialise in heavy-duty plastering for warehouses, factories, and large industrial complexes.

We work within your operational schedule to minimise downtime, using materials and techniques suited for high-demand industrial settings.

  • Warehouse & factory finishes
  • Impact-resistant surfaces
  • Moisture & chemical resistant plaster
  • Large surface area application
  • Acoustic plaster systems
